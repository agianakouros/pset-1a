The Ultimate Sport

For years since its invention, snowboarding has been hailed as one of the greatest extreme sports of all time. It's extremely hard to learn and even harder to master, but it can lead you to some of the most fun you will ever have in your life. Read more about the sport on this site.

What is Snowboarding?

Essentially, snowboarding is the cooler, younger brother of skiing. Like skiing, snowboarding involves going to a mountain with the right gear, buying a lift ticket, taking the lift to the top of the mountain and finding your way down at a pace directly relevant to your skill level. The whole activity is very safe if done correctly and at a non-expert level.
